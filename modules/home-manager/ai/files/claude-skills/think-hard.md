---
name: think-hard
description: Deep analysis mode. Use when facing a complex bug, design decision, or problem that needs careful step-by-step reasoning before acting.
---

Stop and think deeply before doing anything.

Process:
1. **Restate the problem** in your own words to confirm understanding
2. **List what you know** — read relevant code, check error messages, gather facts
3. **Identify what you don't know** — what assumptions are you making?
4. **Consider at least 3 hypotheses** for what's going on
5. **Design an experiment** to distinguish between them
6. **Only then act** — run the most informative test first

Do NOT jump to a fix. The goal is understanding, not speed.
